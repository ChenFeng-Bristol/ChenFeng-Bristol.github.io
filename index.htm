About

I am a currently PhD student in Visual Information Laboratory within the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, University of Bristol under the supervision of Prof. David Bull and Dr Aaron zhang. My main research interests include video processing and video quality assessment.

I obtained MSc (Distiction) in image and video communication and signal processing from University of Bristol, Where I did reasearch on Video Compression with CNN-based Post Processing in Visual Information Laboratory under the supervision of Prof. David Bull. I completed BEng in Measuring and Control Technology at School of Automation and Electrical Engineering in University of Science and Technology Beijing under the supervision of Prof. Qing Li.

My current research is mainly focused on deep video quality assessment and deep video compression.
