About

I am a currently PhD student in Visual Information Laboratory within the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, University of Bristol under the supervision of Prof. David Bull and Dr Aaron zhang. My main research interests include video compression and quality assessment.

Primary Research Areas and Projects

    • Video Compression with CNN-based Post Processing.
